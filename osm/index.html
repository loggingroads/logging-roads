Task: Tracing Roads in OpenStreetMap

Who Can Participate?

Anyone with a computer and internet connection can contribute. However, tracing roads does require learning to use OpenStreetMap. Check out the Mapping Guide for thorough instructions on the editing process, then get started.

Start Mapping

To contribute, click on a country below and choose a single grid task from the Tasking Manager. Editing is handled by the Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team's Tasking Manager.

For more detailed instructions, check out the project Mapping Guide.
